Folkways Album Cover

The Smithsonian Folkways is a collection of music and sounds across generations that preserves music and life into the future. The goal of this project was to use illustration to create an album cover for a selection of folkways records. The selected music was a duo of blue jazz and ragtime jazz. The colorful shapes illustrated onto the album cover were a play on synesthesia. The visualization of the music being bright and sharp for the ragtime contrasts with the curvilinear and blue colors for the blues. This concept was combined with a typeface I created specifically for this project named Bodoni Jazz. The final product is a colorful album cover that invites the owner of such a record to step into the past of jazz.

With an identity established I went through several iterations before landing on a finished logo. The final mark began as an abstract use of the letter S in four directions. The logo consists of 4 curved teardrop shaped abstract marks. The abstract mark can be seen as small ghosts or a camera shutter utilizing the circular whitespace in the marks center. I chose Century Gothic for the type because the equality and symmetry between the forms gave a solid sophisticated form.
